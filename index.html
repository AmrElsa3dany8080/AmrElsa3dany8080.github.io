About Me

Crafting spaces that speak for themselves.

Amr Elsaadany is a Cairo-based interior designer with a passion for transforming spaces into immersive experiences. His work spans residential interiors, luxury retail environments, media studios, and bespoke bathrooms - each project a study in material, light, and intentional living.

With a foundation from the Modern University for Technology & Information (MTI) and hands-on experience at Perfect Company for Design and Contracting, Amr approaches every brief by listening first - then designing with conviction.
